Working alongside the Scientific Registry of Transplant Recipients, developed a platform that empowers patients with clear, accessible educational resources. Featuring interactive tools like a transplant journey map and informative videos, this app enhances patient engagement and supports informed decision-making throughout their healthcare journey.

The preview of this website not only introduces a more intuitive user interface but also sets the stage for future enhancements to professional content on srtr.org, reinforcing SRTR’s commitment to being a trusted, patient-centric resource in the transplant community.
