I collaborated with biostatisticians, doctors, and researchers at the Scientific Registry of Transplant Recipients to develop interactive data visualization reports on organ procurement organization (OPO) performance. These reports transform complex data into accessible insights, allowing users to explore key metrics, compare OPOs, and understand trends in organ procurement practices. By integrating SRTR’s research and expertise, the project supports transparency and drives improvements in the field of organ donation and transplantation.
