The second week in a row people took the street to protest the ongoing ICE operation in the Minneapolis and in other parts of Minnesota. Just like the <a href="/photos/minneapolis-anti-ice-march-january-23">first march</a> the mood was upbeat, powerful and extremely peaceful. All that in the face of another protester being killed, this time it was <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Killing_of_Alex_Pretti">Alex Pretti</a>.
