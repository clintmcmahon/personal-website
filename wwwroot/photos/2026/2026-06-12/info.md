I took a lunchtime walk across downtown Minneapolis to get a banh mi. The banh mi was delicious and I ended up eating it in the IDS Center. Along the way I took some photos of the journey from my coworking space to the IDS Center. 

The sky was a mix of overcast one minute with some spots of sun the next. I didn't get many of the shadow images that I like downtown for, but I was able to snap a couple that turned out pretty well.
