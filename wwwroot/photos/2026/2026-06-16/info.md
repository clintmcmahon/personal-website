I took the Ricoh GRiiix to the Kingfield farmers market this weekend. I discovered that the Ricoh community is really into Fuji Film simulations and have created all these great recipes for the Ricoh. 

These photos were all shot using the Bennet 800 color recipe. I love how deep and rich the colors are in this recipe. It's going to be my default for the summer.
