Last night I took a walk down Lyndale Avenue in the Whittier neighborhood. It's been a long time since I've walked around this area of town. But last night I had a few hours to do nothing so I took my 55-200mm lens out. Normally I use this lens for spotting planes, but it's also fun to take the zoom out for street photography as well.
