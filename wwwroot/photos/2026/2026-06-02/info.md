I took my dog to the airport dog park last week with my Canon T6i. We both enjoy this dog park a lot. She gets to run and lay in the grass and I get to take photos of airplanes. Together we could spend hours and hours there. On this trip I got to catch an Air France 787 landing over another spotter. I didn't know Air France flew the 787 to Minneapolis. The only plane I've noticed them flying here is their 777. It was a cool plane to catch - as well as a good amount of 757s. 

I was also lucky enough to see the new Sun Country retro livery. This plane is super cool to see in person and I like it so much more than the current Sun Country colors.
