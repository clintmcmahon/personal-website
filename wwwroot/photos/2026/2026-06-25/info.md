Saturday we went to the Stone Arch fest along the river by the Stone Arch Bridge. I like to go to this festival every year and talk to the new owner of our <a href="https://northmade.co">old t-shirt company</a>. It's fun to see the new designs they've created and what they are doing with the company since we left. 

We walked up and down the river, stopping at artists booths and getting some ice cream at the base of the bridge. The weather was pretty spectacular - mid 70s. It made for a pretty ideal afternoon with the fam.
