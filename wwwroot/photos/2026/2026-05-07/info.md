A Spring wink of sunshine through the trees. This morning when I took my kiddo to school I was still wearing my winter hat. It's May 7 and I'm still wearing my winter hat. My full winter coat hasn't been put into storage yet. Right now it's still sitting on the top of the stairs in a sort of staging area waiting to get put away for the next two seasons. Before I know it, it's going to be 90 and humid. Winter goes to Summer really fast in this part of the world.
