<p>Last weekend we took a long weekend trip to Palm Springs for some sunshine and warm weather. We split up Saturday to be part pool time and the other part we drove out to Joshua Tree National Park. The drive to the west entrance from Palm Springs is a little over an hour. We left late afternoon in order to catch the sunset and do a little bit of star gazing after the sun went down. </p><p>Joshua Tree is a great national park.
